About Me

Hello there! My name is Owen Liversidge and I am a Full Stack Web Developer. My interest in design and functionality stretches back to my late teenage years, when I studied design in England. After graduating in Design, I moved into the field of music for nearly 20 years. During this time I self-published a series of method books for teaching the drum set, which sell all over the world. During this process my interest in design was reignited and I decided to go back to school to become a Web Developer. The immersive program at General Assembly, in Atlanta, taught me everything that I needed to know (plus more).

Moving forward, I would love to continue to work in the field of media. That way I can combine all of my skills and be of maximum benefit to an individual or company. Eventually, I would love to be a Product Manager and help lead a team to build something truly amazing.

Projects

I created the Trumpisms™ game for my first project at General Assembly, Atlanta. It is based on the classic Hangman game, where you have to guess the word that The Donald once made up. However, if you guess incorrectly, The Donald will start to appear. If you guess too many incorrect letters then you will be fired!
